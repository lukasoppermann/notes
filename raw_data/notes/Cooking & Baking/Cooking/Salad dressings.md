# Tangy Balsamic-Dijon Dressing
- **Flavor Profile:** Sweet, tangy, and robust.
- **Best for:** Heartier greens (kale, spinach, romaine) and grain bowls.
**Instructions:** Whisk all ingredients thoroughly in a small bowl until fully combined. Store in an airtight container for up to 5 days. Shake well before using.

|**Ingredient**|**Quantity**|**Notes**|
|---|---|---|
|Balsamic Vinegar|$1/4$ cup|Essential for flavor and body.|
|Water or Vegetable Broth|$2$ tbsp|Used to thin the dressing without adding calories.|
|Dijon Mustard|$1$ tsp|Acts as an emulsifier to keep the dressing from separating.|
|Maple Syrup (or honey)|$1/2$ tsp (optional)|Use minimally to balance the vinegar's acidity.|
|Garlic Powder|$1/4$ tsp|Use fresh minced garlic for a stronger flavor.|
|Salt and Black Pepper|To taste|
# Zesty Southwest Cilantro-Lime Vinaigrette
- **Flavor Profile:** Bright, fresh, and slightly spicy.
- **Best for:** Taco salads, black bean bowls, or as a marinade for tofu/tempeh.
**Instructions:** Combine all ingredients in a blender or food processor. Blend until the cilantro is finely chopped and the dressing is smooth. Store refrigerated for up to 3 days.

| **Ingredient**                | **Quantity**      | **Notes**                           |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Lime Juice (freshly squeezed) | $1/4$ cup         | The primary acidic base.            |
| Vegetable Broth               | $2$ tbsp          | Adds liquid without calories/fat.   |
| Cilantro (fresh)              | $1/4$ cup, packed | Essential for the Southwest flavor. |
| Cumin                         | $1/2$ tsp         |                                     |
| Onion Powder                  | $1/4$ tsp         |                                     |
| Pinch of Red Pepper Flakes    | Optional          | For a subtle heat.                  |

### Creamy Lemon-Herb Yogurt Dressing
- **Flavor Profile:** Rich, cooling, and herbaceous (similar to a Ranch or Tzatziki base).
- **Best for:** Dipping raw vegetables, topping baked potatoes, or dressing pasta salads.
**Instructions:** Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Start with $1$ tsp of water and add more as needed to reach a pourable consistency. Store refrigerated for up to 4 days.

|**Ingredient**|**Quantity**|**Notes**|
|---|---|---|---|
|Non-Fat Greek Yogurt|$1/2$ cup|High protein base.||
|Lemon Juice|$1$ tbsp|Adds brightness and helps thin the yogurt slightly.||
|Fresh Dill or Chives (chopped)|$1$ tsp|Use dried herbs if fresh are unavailable, but use $1/3$ the amount.||
|Garlic (minced)|$1/2$ clove|||
|Water or Milk (skim/almond)|$1$ tsp (or more)|Thin until desired consistency is reached.||
|Salt and Pepper|To taste|||

# High-Protein Curry-Turmeric Sauce
- **Flavor Profile:** Earthy, savory, and warm with mild spice.
- **Best for:** Drizzling over lentil/chickpea bowls or roasted vegetables (broccoli, chickpeas).
**Instructions:** Whisk all ingredients until completely smooth. Taste and adjust seasoning/sweetener. Store refrigerated for up to 4 days.

|**Ingredient**|**Quantity**|**Notes**|
|---|---|---|
|Non-Fat Greek Yogurt|$1/2$ cup|The creamy base.|
|Curry Powder|$1$ tsp||
|Turmeric Powder|$1/2$ tsp|Adds color and earthy flavor.|
|Apple Cider Vinegar (or water)|$1$ tbsp|Helps thin the sauce for drizzling.|
|Touch of Sweetener (e.g., maple)|$1/4$ tsp (optional)|To balance the spices.|
|Salt|To taste||

## Low-Calorie Tahini-Yogurt Sauce (High Protein)

This recipe heavily dilutes the tahini with yogurt, lemon juice, and water to maximize volume and maintain a smooth texture without excessive fat.

|**Ingredient**|**Quantity**|**Notes**|
|---|---|---|
|Non-Fat Greek Yogurt (Plain)|$1/2$ cup|The high-protein, low-fat base.|
|Tahini (Sesame Paste)|$1$ tsp|Measured precisely to control fat/calories.|
|Lemon Juice (freshly squeezed)|$2$ tbsp|Essential for cutting the richness of the tahini.|
|Water (or vegetable broth)|$1$ to $2$ tbsp|Used to thin the sauce to a pourable consistency.|
|Garlic (minced)|$1/2$ clove (or $1/4$ tsp powder)||
|Cumin|$1/4$ tsp|Classic pairing with tahini/yogurt.|
|Salt and Black Pepper|To taste|
