### Ingredients for 4 waffles
- **1** Large egg (approx. 50g–55g)
- **55g** Spelt 1050 flour
- **220g** Shredded root vegetables (squeeze out excess moisture)
- **55g** Grated cheese
- **15g** Green onions (1 onion)
- **3g** Salt (plus pepper to taste)
- **10ml** Water or milk (optional, only if the batter is too stiff)

## High-Protein Veggie Waffles (4 Waffles)
- **1** Large egg
- **60g** Spelt 1050 flour
- **100g** Low-fat quark (Magerquark) or thick Greek yogurt
- **200g** Shredded root vegetables (squeeze out all liquid)
- **30g** Hemp seeds (shelled) or linen seeds
- **60g** Grated hard cheese (e.g., Parmesan or aged Gouda for higher protein)
- **15g** Green onions, chopped
- **Salt, pepper, and nutmeg** to taste

---
## Instructions
1. **Mix:** Whisk the egg & seeds in a bowl and let it sit for 3-5 min. 
2. **Prep:** Grate the vegetables and squeeze them in a clean kitchen towel to remove excess liquid. This prevents the waffles from becoming soggy. 
3. **Add** the 55g of Spelt flour and salt to egg mix. Stir until a thick, uniform paste forms.
4. **Combine:** Fold in the shredded vegetables, cheese, and herbs. The mixture should be thick and coat the vegetables thoroughly.
5. **Timing:** Cook for 6–8 minutes. Because of the Spelt 1050 and the DuraCeramic plates, the waffles will brown more evenly but may require an extra minute to crisp the center.
6. **Extraction:** Lift gently with a silicone spatula. The DuraCeramic coating should release the waffle easily once the steam stops escaping.
