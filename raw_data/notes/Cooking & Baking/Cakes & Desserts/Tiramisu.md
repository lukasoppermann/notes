## Ingredients

| Ingredient     | Medium sized form          |
| -------------- | -------------------------- |
| Eggs           | 2                          |
| Mascarpone     | 250g                       |
| Cream          | 200g                       |
| Sugar          | 25g                        |
| Vanilla        | A bit                      |
| Cocoa powder   | For covering the top       |
| Almond flavour | 3 drops                    |
| Rum flavour    | 3 drops                    |
| Espresso       | 150ml (3 Espresso + Water) |
| Löffelbiskuit  | For 2 layers               |
## Instructions
1. Separate eggs
2. Whisk egg-white until firm and refrigerate
3. Whisk cream with vanilla and refrigerate
4. Mix egg yolk, sugar and aromas until creamy
5. In a new bowl, mix mascarpone until creamy
6. Add egg yolk mix to mascarpone and incorporate
7. Carefully **using a spatula** add the cream from the fridge to mascarpone (unterheben)
8. Carefully **using a spatula** add egg-white mix  to mascarpone (unterheben)
9. Assemble:
	1. Dip Löffelbiskuit from both sides into coffee
	2. Place with sugary side up in the form
	3. Add first layer of cream mix until half is left
	4. Add second layer of Löffelbiskuit
	5. Add the test of the cream mix
	6. Cover top with cocoa power
	7. Refrigerate for 2-3h or even better over night
