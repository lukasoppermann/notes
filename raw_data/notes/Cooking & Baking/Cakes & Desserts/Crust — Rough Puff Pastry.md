## Ingredients

| Ingredient              | 28cm | 10x7cm |
| ----------------------- | ---- | ------ |
| Flour 550               | 250g | 200g   |
| Butter                  | 190g | 152g   |
| Salt                    | 2.8g | pinch  |
| Sugar                   | 14g  | 11g    |
| Iced Water              | 132g | 105g   |
| **For chocolate dough** |      |        |
| Cocoa                   | 30g  | 24g    |
| Additional sugar        | 7g   | 6g     |

## Instructions

1. Freeze the butter for 10-15min
2. In a large bowl mix the flour (+ cocoa), sugar and salt and place in freezer until butter is firm
3. Grate butter into flour (using a glove) an use fork to toss
4. Drizzle in half of the water and carefully mix with fork
5. Add more of the water until it comes together a bit
6. Carefully press together to a big ball
7. Place on plastic wrap and flatten
8. Fold plastic wrap over and flatten a bit more with a rolling pin to get dough in all corners
9. Refrigerate for 2 hours
