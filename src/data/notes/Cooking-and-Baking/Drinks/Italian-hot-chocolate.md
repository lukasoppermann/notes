title: "Italian hot chocolate"
---

| Ingredients | 4 small cups | 2 small cups |
| ----------- | ------------ | ------------ |
| Milk        | 300ml        | 150ml        |
| Cocoa       | 30g          | 15g          |
| Sugar       | 10g          | 5g           |
| Corn starch | 10g          | 5g           |
| Chocolate   | 30g          | 15g          |

## Instructions
1. Mix the cocoa powder, sugar, corn starch, pinch of salt and vanilla in a pot with a whisk
2. Slow add some milk and whisk together
3. Once it is a bit fluid, place on low heat and add milk while stirring
4. Cut chocolate into small pieces and add to mixture
5. Continue cooking while stirring until it thickens (it will continue to ticken while it cools down)
